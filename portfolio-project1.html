Project#1 - Pick your Plate

Instructions

This is Project#1. Use what was learned through week 1-8 and develop an app.

Get Started!

Our site allows users to find recipes to cook at home or find restaurants to eat out. Recipes can be searched by a dish name, an ingredient, or a keyword. Once recipe search is performed, more information can be obtained by clicking on the image. Recipes can be marked as favorite by clicking on the Like icon. Restaurants can be searched by filling out all the parameters as shown. Address/PhoneNumber can be obtained by clicking on the image. Directions to the restaurant can be obtained by clicking on the map icon. Yelp page of the restaurant can be accessed by clicking on the web icon.

Enjoy cooking!
